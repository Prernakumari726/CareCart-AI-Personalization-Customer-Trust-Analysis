This project analyzes the impact of AI-driven personalization on customer behavior and trust for a D2C wellness & personal care company (CareCart).

## Objectives
- Evaluate pre-AI vs post-AI performance
- Identify trust-sensitive customer segments
- Design AI vs Human decision framework
- Propose trust-preserving strategy and KPIs

## Tools Used
- Python (data cleaning & preparation)
- Excel (Pivot tables, KPIs, dashboard)

## Key Insights
- 74% of revenue now comes from post-AI period, indicating growth from AI personalization.
- Trust Sensitivity Index = 0.60 showing high privacy concern.
- High escalation rates in sensitive touchpoints require human oversight.

## Recommendations
- Implement AI disclosure labels and opt-in/opt-out controls.
- Use human agents for privacy-sensitive interactions.
- Monitor trust KPIs alongside revenue KPIs.
